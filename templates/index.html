Who We Are

The Telugu Association of Nigeria (TAN) is a vibrant cultural organization dedicated to preserving the rich heritage of Telugu people in Nigeria.

  • We foster unity through cultural events, educational support, and community services.
  • Our members represent diverse professions united by shared Telugu traditions.

Our Impact

We bring people together through culture, language, and community.
